## About Me

My background is in cloud infrastructure, security engineering, and governance, risk, and compliance (GRC). I focus on translating regulatory and policy requirements into practical technical controls within modern cloud environments.

Currently, I work on building automated compliance systems using cloud services such as AWS Config, EventBridge, Lambda, and Systems Manager. My goal is to design systems that continuously detect policy violations, trigger remediation, and generate reliable audit evidence without heavy manual effort.

I am passionate about the emerging field of GRC engineering, where compliance is built directly into infrastructure. I believe controls should be automated, continuously verified, and demonstrable, helping organizations maintain trust and security while still enabling innovation.
